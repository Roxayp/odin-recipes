General Tao Tofu Recipe

Sweet, tangy crispy tofu recipe

Try this quick and easy recipe to give a good rep to tofu

Ingredients

For the sauce :
  • 1/2 cup veggie broth
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp sambal oelek
For the stir-fry :
  • 450g firm tofu
  • 2 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup veggie oil
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped

Steps

For the sauce :
  1. In a bowl, combine all the ingredients. Set aside.
For the stir-fry :
  1. In a large bowl, dredge the tofu in the cornstarch. Shake to remove any excess and set aside on a plate.
  2. In a wok over high heat, heat the oil. Fry half of the tofu at a time until lightly browned. Season with salt and pepper. Using a slotted spoon, remove the tofu from the wok and drain on paper towels.
  3. In the same wok, fry the onions, ginger and garlic for 1 to 2 minutes. Add oil, if needed. Add the sauce and bring to a boil. Add the tofu and cook, stirring to warm through, and coat thoroughly with the sauce. Serve with rice and broccoli.
